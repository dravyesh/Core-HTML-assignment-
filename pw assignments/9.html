Text Formatting in HTML

  1. <b> and <strong>:
    • Use: Emphasizes text as bold.
    • Best Practices: <strong> is semantically stronger than <b> and is preferred for important text.
  2. <i> and <em>:
    • Use: Emphasizes text as italic.
    • Best Practices: <em> is semantically stronger than <i> and is preferred for emphasized text.
  3. <u>:
    • Use: Underlines text.
    • Best Practices: Avoid using underlines for non-link text to prevent confusion with hyperlinks.
  4. <sub> and <sup>:
    • Use: Subscripts and superscripts, respectively.
    • Best Practices: Use <sub> for subscript (e.g., chemical formulas), and <sup> for superscript (e.g., exponents).
  5. <mark>:
    • Use: Highlights text with a yellow background to indicate a portion of content.
    • Best Practices: Use for highlighting search terms or other relevant content.
